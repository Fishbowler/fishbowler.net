This week:

* Kent Dodds reminded us to [test our tests](https://kentcdodds.com/blog/make-your-test-fail) by amending the implementation to force a fail, else risk inadvertently testing the wrong thing and not spotting when the _real_ thing breaks.
* An interesting conversation about BrowserStack vs the agency model. Sometimes projects have no need for cross-browser testing (for example, a corporate application used with a single browser), and sometimes projects have no GUI at all. This model of work can get in the way of ROI for annual-investment SaaS (which admittedly, BrowserStack isn't).
